TORNADO ALLEY

This is the second section

Tornado Alley is the name given to a group of states (shown in the chart on the left) where most of the tornados in the country occur. This is because of its location as hot and cool winds more frequently meet to eventually form tornados. When filtering to a state, vertical lines of circles appear on the cchart. This is because tornadoes often form in clusters.
